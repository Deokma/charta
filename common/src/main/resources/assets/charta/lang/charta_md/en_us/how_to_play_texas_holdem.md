### **Objective:**

Have the most chips when all other players run out, or win the current pot by having the best hand at showdown.

### **Setup:**

**Players:** 2–8 players

**Deck:** Standard 52-card deck (Spades, Hearts, Clubs, Diamonds)

- Each player starts with the same number of chips (configurable).
- A **Dealer** button rotates clockwise each hand.
- The two players to the left of the dealer post forced bets called **blinds**.

### **Game Flow:**

Each hand has four betting rounds:

1. **Pre-Flop** — Each player receives 2 private (hole) cards. Betting starts left of the big blind.
2. **Flop** — 3 community cards are revealed face-up. Betting starts left of the dealer.
3. **Turn** — 1 more community card is revealed. Another betting round.
4. **River** — The 5th and final community card is revealed. Final betting round.
5. **Showdown** — If more than one player remains, hands are revealed and the best 5-card hand wins the pot.

### **Betting Actions:**

- **Check** — Pass without betting (only when no bet is owed).
- **Call** — Match the current bet.
- **Raise** — Increase the bet by a minimum of one big blind.
- **All-In** — Bet all your remaining chips.
- **Fold** — Discard your hand and forfeit the round.

### **Hand Rankings (best to worst):**

1. **Royal Flush** — A K Q J 10 of the same suit
2. **Straight Flush** — Five consecutive cards of the same suit
3. **Four of a Kind** — Four cards of the same rank
4. **Full House** — Three of a kind + a pair
5. **Flush** — Five cards of the same suit
6. **Straight** — Five consecutive cards
7. **Three of a Kind** — Three cards of the same rank
8. **Two Pair** — Two different pairs
9. **One Pair** — Two cards of the same rank
10. **High Card** — Highest single card

### **Additional:**

- The game continues for multiple hands until only one player has chips remaining.
- Game options (starting chips, big blind amount) can be configured before the game starts.
